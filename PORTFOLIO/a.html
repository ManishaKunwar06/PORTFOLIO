MOVIE REVIEW


Persuit of Happiness

In this blog I am going to review the movie called The Pursuit of happiness. I am going to answer some of the questions here.

Q1 : Your favourite Dialogue and why?

Answer : After watching the movie The Pursuit of Happiness the dialogue that become my favourite is when Will Smith (Christopher Gardner) the main character of the movie was in an interview and he tells the interviewer that "I am a kind of person who if does not know the answer I am going to tell you that I do not know the answer but I bet you I am going to find the answer and I know how to find the answer". I like this dialogue because this dialogue shows that he has believe in himself and he is also fully confident of his capability.

Q2 : Your Personal Review ?

Answer : According to me this movie is one of the finest of all time. This movie show us the difficult phase of life and how one can overcome that phase by just working hard and pushing his or her limits. This movie has drama , emotions , comedy and everything which needs to be there . I believe this movie should be watched by everyone once in their span of life . If one looses their perspective towards life than this movie should be watched.

Q3 : Learning and Take away from the movie.

Answer : There are many learnings from the movie called The Pursuit of happiness , some of are : The first thing that I have learned from the movie is attitude of "never give up" . As shown in the move how a common man fight against all the odds in life and overcome those situations just by never give up attitude. The second thing that I have learned is "never give up on your dream" as shown in the movie Christopher Gardner the main character in the movie how he went from worst to worst situations and still come up with the same energy and try to achieve his dream . That is why we saw at the end of the movie how he became successful in achieving his dream . The third thing that I have learned from the movie is "To be Kind" .
